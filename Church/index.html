About Our Ministry

Rooted in prayer, established in faith, and transforming the community of Ndazabazadde.

OUR ORIGINS

The Journey of Faith

NDAZABAZADDE ISRAEL MINISTRIES was established with a clear calling: to raise an altar of prayer, deliverance, and true worship in Gayaza Manyangwa, Uganda. Under the divine direction of Apostle Kawuki Elifazi and his wife Margrete Kawuki, the ministry began as a small fellowship of believers hungry for the raw power and truth of God’s Word.

Over the years, the ministry has grown into a spiritual lighthouse, helping individuals break free from spiritual bondages, restoring broken families, and raising dedicated disciples of Jesus Christ. The name "Ndazabazadde" represents a place of fruitfulness and spiritual birthing, where souls are gathered and nurtured to inherit the promises of God.

Our Vision

To see a completely transformed generation, walking in righteousness, operating in the power of the Holy Spirit, and reflecting the glory of Israel's God in every sphere of life.

Our Mission

To preach the undiluted Gospel of Jesus Christ, engage in intense intercessory prayer, offer biblical discipleship, and perform community outreaches that express God's love practically.

Core Values

Faith: Total reliance on God.
Holiness: Pursuing Christ-like purity.
Love: Serving with compassion.
Prayer: Maintaining constant communion.
Unity: Fellowship as one family.

Statement of Faith

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, serving as our supreme rule of faith and conduct.

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three Persons: God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit.

Salvation by Grace

We believe that salvation is received solely through faith in the sacrificial death and resurrection of Jesus Christ, apart from human works.

Power of the Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism and present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, including spiritual gifts, divine healing, miracles, and deliverance.

Our Spiritual Leadership

Meet the founders and spiritual directors who shepherd Ndazabazadde Israel Ministries with integrity, passion, and love.

Apostle Kawuki Elifazi

Founder & Lead Overseer

Apostle Elifazi is a seasoned minister of the Gospel with a powerful apostolic grace. He is known for his deep commitment to intercessory prayer, prophetic insight, and a ministry characterized by deliverance and raw miracles. His passion is to see souls set free from bondage.

Co-Founder & Lead Pastor

Pastor Margrete Kawuki works hand-in-hand with her husband to shepherd the flock. She leads the Women's Ministry and the Intercessory Prayer Desk. Known for her motherly heart, wisdom, and counseling grace, she has raised many families in the fear of the Lord.

Weekly Fellowship Times

08:00 AM - 01:00 PM

Sunday Worship & Deliverance Service

Our main weekly service featuring Morning Glory prayer, Christian discipleship teaching, powerful praise, deep worship, apostolic preaching, prophetic declarations, and deliverance ministry.

Tuesdays: 05:00 PM - 07:00 PM

Cell Groups & Home Fellowship

Small group Bible studies held in various community zones (Manyangwa, Gayaza, etc.) for close fellowship and mutual care.

Wednesdays: 07:00 AM - 12:00 PM (noon)

Counselling & Deliverance Service

A dedicated mid-week session for personal spiritual counselling, consultation, and individual deliverance prayers with the pastors. Please note that entries close strictly at 12:00 PM (noon).

Wednesdays: 02:00 PM - 05:00 PM

Mid-Week Fellowship & Bible Study Service

A corporate mid-week service featuring praise and worship, testimonies of miracles, deep biblical study teachings, and collective prayers for breakthrough.

Every First 5 Days of a Month: 06:00 AM - 07:00 AM

Monthly Prayer Altar

A covenant start to the month, dedicated to establishing family and personal prayer altars, fasting, and seeking God's guidance and blessings for the days ahead.

Second Friday of Every Month: 09:00 PM - 04:00 AM

Monthly Deliverance Night Vigil

Our main corporate overnight service. A night of intense spiritual warfare, praise, deliverance, and prophetic declarations for families, the church, and the nation.

Saturdays: 02:00 PM - 05:00 PM

Youth Fellowship & Choir Rehearsal

Specialized youth program and praise team preparation for Sunday service.

Special Campaigns & Outreach

Evangelism Crusades

Outdoor evangelistic campaigns in villages and trading centers around Kayunga and Gayaza, spreading the gospel message and saving souls.

Community Outreach

Providing food relief, clothes, clean water initiatives, and basic counseling to needy families in Ndazabazadde.

Annual Revivals

Special multi-day conferences featuring guest ministers, drawing thousands of believers for spiritual renewal and fire.
